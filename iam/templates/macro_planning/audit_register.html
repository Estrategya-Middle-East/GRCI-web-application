Instructions

  1. Collect all available organizational data, including process maps, charts, and departmental records.
  2. Identify all departments, systems, processes, and locations that can be audited.
  3. Categorize entities into groups such as operational, financial, IT, and compliance.
  4. Assign risk levels to each entity using historical findings and criticality to business objectives.
  5. Validate identified entities by consulting department heads or stakeholders.
  6. Ensure no significant entity is missed by cross-referencing organizational documents.
  7. Schedule audits based on risk levels and organizational priorities.
  8. Use software or templates to document entities and assign priorities.
  9. Prepare a draft of the Audit Universe and circulate it for feedback.
  10. Finalize the Audit Universe and record it in the Audit Universe Form.
